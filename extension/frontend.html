Mindctrl depends on external MQTT server to communicate with clients. Therefore, Mindctrl needs to know the information about the MQTT server, as well as the name it uses to identify itself to clients.

Note that saving the preferences here does not affect running service. The saved preferences will only be applied after a restart.
